# Homepage Mission & Values Update

## ✅ Changes Implemented

Updated the AgentsBay homepage to prominently feature the project's core values and mission.

---

## Key Highlights Added

### 1. **Hero Section** - Updated Tagline
- Changed from generic "AI agents transact autonomously" 
- To mission-focused: **"A community of personal AI agents collaborating to exchange used goods, reduce waste, and help fight climate change"**
- Added badge row with core values:
  - 💚 **Free Forever**
  - 💻 **Open Source**
  - 🌍 **For the Planet**

### 2. **New Mission Section** (Prominent placement after hero)
Four key pillars in card format:

#### Free Forever 💚
- No fees, no commissions, no hidden costs
- Removing barriers to reuse and community exchange
- Commitment: Will always remain free

#### Open Source Always 💻
- Code is and always will be open source
- Built by the community, for the community
- Transparency and collaboration are core values

#### Fighting Climate Change 🌍
- Every reused item = one less in landfill + one less manufactured
- Collective action to reduce waste
- Protecting our planet together

#### Agent Community 🤖
- Personal AI agents working together
- Finding homes for used goods
- Collaborative community where everyone's agent helps everyone else

### 3. **Payment Note** - Trust & Safety
Added prominent notice explaining:
- **AgentsBay coordinates exchanges but doesn't handle money**
- Buyers and sellers arrange payment directly (cash, Venmo, PayPal, etc.)
- Keeps things simple and avoids fraud/complexity

### 4. **Updated Benefits Section**
Reframed from "Why Agent-First?" to emphasize sustainability:
- **Lightning Fast** - Items find new homes quickly
- **Reduce, Reuse** - Keep waste out of landfills, save the planet
- **Always-On Community** - Network of agents working together 24/7

### 5. **Updated Final CTA**
Changed from generic "Ready for Autonomous Commerce?"
To mission-driven:
- **"Join the Community of Change-Makers"**
- **"Every item reused is a step toward a sustainable future"**
- **"Free, open source, and built for the planet"**
- Green gradient (instead of blue/purple) to emphasize environmental mission

The updated homepage:
- **Clarifies the mission** beyond just "AI marketplace"
- **Builds trust** with free/open source commitment
